Design Rationale

Why This Palette?

Royal Purple (#5B3A8F): Conveys creativity, wisdom, and sophistication — perfect for a publishing business. It's distinctive without being overwhelming, establishing brand recognition while maintaining professionalism.

Antique Gold (#D4AF37): Adds warmth and prestige. This muted gold feels established and trustworthy rather than flashy. It pairs beautifully with purple and provides elegant accents.

Neutral Grays: The expanded neutral palette (charcoal to off-white) provides hierarchy and breathing room. This allows the purple and gold to make statements without overwhelming the design.

Why These Fonts?

Lora: A contemporary serif specifically optimized for screen readability. Its brushed curves and moderate contrast make it highly legible at all sizes while maintaining warmth and sophistication. Unlike traditional serifs, Lora feels modern and approachable while still honoring literary tradition.

Inter: Exceptional readability and modern feel. It's neutral enough to let content shine while maintaining personality. Pairs perfectly with Cormorant's classical elegance, creating sophisticated contrast between headings and body text.
